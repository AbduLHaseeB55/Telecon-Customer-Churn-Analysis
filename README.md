# 📞 Telco Customer Churn Analysis

## 🎯 Business Objective
Customer churn is a critical metric for telecommunications companies. This project analyzes customer behavior to identify the primary drivers of churn and builds a predictive model to forecast which customers are most likely to leave. By identifying at-risk customers early, the business can proactively dispatch targeted retention offers, directly protecting monthly recurring revenue.

## 📊 Key Business Insights
* **Contract Impact:** Customers on month-to-month plans exhibit a significantly higher churn rate compared to those on 1-year or 2-year commitments.
* **Price Sensitivity:** Higher monthly charges are strongly correlated with increased churn, highlighting price sensitivity in the market.
* **Onboarding & Loyalty:** Churn is heavily concentrated among new customers. Once a customer survives the initial 6 months, their loyalty increases significantly.
* **Tech Support:** Customers without technical support are far more likely to churn, suggesting that technical frustration is a major churn driver.

## 🤖 Machine Learning Model
* **Algorithm:** Random Forest Classifier
* **Performance:** ~78.5% Accuracy
* **Application:** Automated early-warning system for customer retention teams.
